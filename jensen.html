JENSEN ACKLES

Jensen Ross Ackles, better known as simply Jensen Ackles, was born on March 1, 1978, in Dallas, Texas. Jensen grew up in Richardson, Texas.Jensen graduated from Dartmouth Elementary School in 1990, he graduated from Apollo Junior High School in 1993, and LV Berkner High School in 1996.Jensen married Danneel Harris on May 15, 2010 in Dallas, Texas.Their first child, a daughter, was born in May 2013.In August 2016, the couple announced that they were expecting twins, a boy and a girl, later in the year.On December 2, 2016, they welcomed twins, Zeppelin Bram and Arrow Rhodes.
