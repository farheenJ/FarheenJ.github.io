MISHA COLLINS

Misha Collins was born in Boston, Massachusetts, to Richard Krushnic[citation needed] and Rebecca Tippens.He was raised in an irreligious family.He has said that his surname, Krushnic, "goes back six generations in Canada, and we're not sure where they came from."His stage name, Collins, is his great grandmother's maiden name.Before acting, he interned for four months at the White House during the Clinton Administration in the Office of Presidential Personnel.Collins attended Greenfield Center School, Northfield Mount Hermon School and the University of Chicago, where he studied social theory.On October 6, 2001, Collins married Victoria Vantoch, in Maine. They have a son, West Anaximander Collins (born September 23, 2010), and a daughter, Maison Marie Collins (born September 25, 2012).
