S12: Ep7 "Rock Never Dies"

Temporarily fixing his vessel with one of his fossilized angel feathers, Lucifer goes to Los Angeles reviving Vince Vincente's rock career for fresh worshipers. Dean, Sam, Castiel and Crowley try intimidating those in Vince's circle to find out where Lucifer's concert will be held without success. Lucifer's forcing Vince's manager to kill himself gets bandmate Tommy to text Castiel the location. Lucifer kills the rest of the band and beats Castiel and Crowley, though the Winchesters scare the rest of the humans away to safety. Lucifer reveals he has no plan, upset that God apologized to him only to leave again to be with Amara. Now Lucifer believes everything is pointless and he just wants to have fun by making people suffer and forcing his enemies to watch, powerless to stop him. The effort of the battle burns through Vince's body and Lucifer promises to go onto bigger things, escaping. Dean considers saving the crowd a win but Sam says Vince meant a lot to many people and they are just slowly losing without a way to stop Lucifer.
